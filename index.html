Data Modeling and Visualization

Project carried out for the Curricular Unit of Data Engineering to Support Decision Making. Practical project that aims to implement and structure data on a given topic in Data Lakehouse, as well as its analytical analysis. Central theme of the project: The Socioeconomic Situation of a Population and its Impact on Corresponding Health Indicators. This theme was developed and implemented using 1 central dataset and 4 additional datasets related to the same central dataset, with its themes: Population statistics (central dataset); Alcohol - Impact of alcohol consumption in the world; Covid-19 - Deaths and reported cases of covid-19 by country; Unemployment - Unemployment rate over the last 31 years; Minimum wage. Project composed of several data processing stages, including: data quality analysis of a dataset relating to different themes and identification of possible analysis objectives, following a three-stage DeltaLake methodology: bronze, silver and gold. After processing the data and quality of information, three dashboards were developed with the aim of analyzing and interpreting the data sets on the respective themes, in a more efficient and intuitive way.
